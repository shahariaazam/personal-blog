To choose the real Search Engine Optimization (SEO) expert in Bangladesh you should remember the following points-

?How to choose SEO expert for your website??
  • ?Ask few of his/her previous work
  • Discuss about few success stories about his/her SEO work
  • Which guidelines does he/she basically follow?
  • What kinds of result he/she can make or what can be the timeframe?
  • His/her techniques will be White Hat SEO or Black Hat SEO techniques?
  • What are your most favorite techniques?
  • How you analyze keyword?
  • What's your experience in developing international sites?
  • How can you represent my contents in SERP?
  • What types of changes has major search engine have made recently?
  • What's the experience about my industry, country/city?
  • How you will find our competitors? Will you use any tools or will I provide you the information?
  • How can I communicate with you for getting time-to-time update?

Those can be your question if you think you are going to hire a Bangladeshi SEO Expert for your website. Nobody can ensure you the SEO result 100%. But he will win who are updated with the Search Engines.

 

TO BECOME A SUCCESSFUL SEO EXPERT YOU MUST BE A LOVER OF GOOGLE SEARCH. TRY TO SEARCH CONTENT FASTER SO YOU CAN UNDERSTAND HOW GOOGLE IS WORKING FOR YOUR QUERY.
