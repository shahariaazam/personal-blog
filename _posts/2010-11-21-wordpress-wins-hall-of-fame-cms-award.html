It's a great news for all of wordpress lovers that WordPress wins Hall Of Fame CMS Award where Drupal is 1st runner up and Joomla is 2nd runner up. It's really a great news for all wordpress community.

[caption id="attachment_627" align="alignleft" width="414" caption="Facebook wins Hall Of Fame CMS Award 2010"][/caption]

Recently PACKT Publication (publish and give various kinds of awards) published this result. They have given the award in many categories and WordPress has won the Hall Of Fame CMS Award 2010. This category was reserved for those CMSes that have won the Open Source CMS Award at least once in previous years.

So from now WordPress is not only a Blogging system but also a CMS where plugins are increasing it's usability. It's an amazing CMS platform for not only developer but also it is most user friendly for website owners or general users.

After this achievements, wordpress has released a news on their official website where the WordPress authority says that " We don?t want WordPress to develop a reputation."
