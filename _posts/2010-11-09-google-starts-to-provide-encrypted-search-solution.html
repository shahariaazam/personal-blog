It's a great news for them who want extra privacy for their internet usage. Google starts to provide encrypted search solution that will provide you a SSL connections that will help your google search terms and search result page from being intercepted by any third party.? With this extra security layer the communication between your computer and Google's server are more secured. Nobody can intercept your information.

[adsense_id="2"]

To use this feature from now please go to https://encrypted.google.com/ and search terms and get your expected search result page.

For more details please go to http://www.google.com/support/websearch/bin/answer.py?answer=173733&hl=en
