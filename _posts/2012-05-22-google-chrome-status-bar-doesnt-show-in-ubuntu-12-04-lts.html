Today I faced a problem in my UBUNTU netbook that is I can't see my Google Chrome status bar. I just discovered it why it happened. Just right click on your UBUNTU 12.04 TLS desktop and select 'Change Background' and then 'Beahavior' then turn off from the right top position of 'Auto Hide Luncher'.

It will solve the problem.
